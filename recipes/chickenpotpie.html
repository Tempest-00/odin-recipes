Chicken Pot Pie

Description:
An easy recipe to make a quick, hearty Chicken Pot Pie.

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 30 mins Servings: 6

Chicken pot pie is an American classic dish that is loved by many families nationally. Whether it's a thanksgiving dinner, a potluck, or just dinner, there is always sure to be at least one person who's in love with the dish.

With this easy prep recipe for Chicken Pot Pie, you can have the favor of the table, without as much of the hassle that comes in the amount of time it takes to make a potpie.

Ingredients

  • 2 (9 inch) deep dish frozen pie crusts, thawed
  • 1 (15oz) can of mixed vegetables, drained
  • 2 cups cookedm diced chicken breast
  • 1 (11oz) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a bowl, mix together the vegetables, cream of chicken soup, and milk.
  3. Pour mixture into one of the pie crusts, turn the other crust over, and pop it out of the tin onto the top of the filled pie. Seal the edges and poke holes in the top crust for venting.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes or until crust is golden brown.

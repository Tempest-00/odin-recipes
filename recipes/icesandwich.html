Ice Sandwich

Description:
The perfect snack for when it's 3am, and you are digging yourself beneath the surface of rock bottom.

Prep: 4 hours Cook: 30 seconds Servings: 1 if it counts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Access to a freezer

Steps

  1. Place the 1 cup of water in the freezer for about 4 hours, to make sure the ice is nice and crunchy.
  2. After the water is frozen, take a spoon and smash the large piece of ice into smaller pieces in a bowl.
  3. Place desired amount of ice shards onto first slice of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread over the top.

Congratulations! I suppose.

Bowl of Cereal

Description:
Culinary success.

Prep: 1 min Cook 1 min Servings 1

A bowl of cereal is a meal loved by many, and appreciated by those at waking up in penthouses, or under a bridge. This simple dish is a classic known to almost all humankind!

Understanding a bowl of cereal is parallel to understanding oneself, and beware, many will try to learn secret information on you based on what your favorite cereal is. A bowl of cereal is not only a dish, but a symbol.

Ingredients

  • 1-7 cups of Your Favorite Cereal
    • Please select favorite cereal
  • 2 cups of milk

Steps

  1. Pour your desired amount of cereal into a bowl of appropriate size to contain it.
  2. Pour the full 2 cups of milk over the cereal, making sure to pour it over all flakes.
  3. Put spoon in bowl.

Congratulations, you've embarked on a journey of self discovery through ritual of morning nourishment!
